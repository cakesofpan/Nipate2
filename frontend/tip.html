Your privacy

Anonymous tips: No IP, no identity, no tracking. Your information is used only to help find the missing person.

Identified tips: Your contact details are visible only to the assigned investigating officer and senior administrators. Never published.

What makes a good tip

✓ Date and time of sighting

✓ Description of clothing or companions

✓ Vehicle details (plate, colour, type)

✓ Any photos or video if safe to take
