What happens next

1. Your report is submitted and published immediately with a case number.

2. The public and investigators can see the case straight away.

3. Tips from the public are private — only you (the reporter) and police can read them.

4. You will be notified by email of any status changes or new tips.

Your data & privacy

Your ID document is stored in an encrypted private bucket and only accessed by authorised administrators for verification. It is never shared publicly.

Your personal contact details are never shown publicly — only the case details you provide are visible.

Emergency?

If this is an immediate emergency, contact police directly.
