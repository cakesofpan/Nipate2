How Nipate works

1. Report online

Submit a verified report with photos, location on Google Maps, and a detailed description. ID verification ensures report quality.

2. Goes public instantly

Your report is published immediately with a unique case number. The public can search for and share it right away.

3. Alerts sent

Verified cases trigger immediate email alerts to county subscribers and social media sharing to maximise reach.

4. Community tips

Anyone can submit anonymous tips. Each tip is reviewed by assigned officers and tracked through to resolution.
